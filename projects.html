Onboarding & Offboarding

Both the onboarding and offboarding processes were almost nonexistent so I spent a lot of my time implementing a new and improved system. Both processes utilise automation through Power Automate and integrate with Microsoft Form, Freshdesk, Email and Teams.

Onboarding

The hiring manager submits a Microsoft form, triggering an automated Power Automate flow. The flow then alerts IT (in Freshdesk) to create an email account for the new user. Simultaneously, the flow searches repeatedly through Office 365 Admin Centre until it locates the new user account. Once the account is found tickets are created in Freshdesk to alert specific IT team members to create new accounts for the new employee. Throughout this process, both HR & the manager are kept in the loop (through automation) in Teams & Outlook.

Offboarding

The offboarding automation has a very similar path to onboarding automation. It also involves a Microsoft form, triggering a range of Freshdesk tickets which are auto-allocated. It also sends out emails to both HR & the manager to keep them updated with the process.
